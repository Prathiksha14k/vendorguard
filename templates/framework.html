Compliance Framework Alignment

GDPR Article 28 — Data Processor Requirements

Our Compliance Tracker enforces that vendors with sensitive data access (data_sensitivity ≥ 6) hold valid SOC2/ISO27001/GDPR DPA certifications. Missing or expired certs trigger EXPIRED_CERTIFICATION anomalies.

GDPR Article 33 — 72-Hour Breach Notification

Recent breaches (within 12 months) on vendors with sensitive access are flagged BREACHED_VENDOR_HIGH_ACCESS at CRITICAL severity, surfaced immediately on the Alerts page so the 72-hour notification clock can be managed.

NIST SP 800-53 SA-9 — Third-Party Service Security

Every vendor has a last_assessment_date; assessments overdue by 365+ days trigger automatic alerts, enforcing regular third-party review cycles.

SOX 404 — Control Dependencies on Third Parties

The Vendor Detail page's score breakdown chart makes each control dependency (data sensitivity, access scope, compliance gaps, financial health) auditable and explainable — supporting internal control evaluation.
